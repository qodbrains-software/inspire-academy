ABOUT US

Who are we?

Inspire Academy is an education institution that exists for the cause of inspiring people to pursue and happily engage in endeavors that fulfill and inspire them. Moreover, Inspire Academy is ran by two fully competent individuals, between them is stupendous amounts of experience in tutoring, business leadership and management, and exellent human interaction and management.

Our Vision

We as Inspire Academyy strive to be leaders in the cause of inspiring people through education and fulfillment of their purposes. This is to be accomplished by establishing an academy that will be accessible to anyone who believes in our cause. We plan to alter the existing eco-system between the students and tertiary institutions and create an eco-system that will result in a greater influx of students in tertiary institutions.

Our Motive

Inspire Academy exists for a far greater cause than education and tutoring, we believe in a world where every single individual pursuing engaging in endeavors that fulfill them and inspires them.
