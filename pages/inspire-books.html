Book Description

Maths Simplified deconstructs and simplifies Maths to its core basics to enable learners to build a solid foundation of understanding so that they can reach their full potential and achieve excellence in Maths. Colour-coded notes, step-by-step worked examples, and 160 carefully selected exam questions make learning and understanding Maths much easier.

In this book, students will find other special features for easier learning, such as:

  • Key concepts to know and practice
  • Useful advice
  • Definitions and explanations
  • Information to highlight
  • Information to recall
  • Key information and steps
  • The flow of calculations
  • Past examinations in each section for exam-readiness practice

The book is easy to use and when studied diligently and with focus, will fully prepare Grade 12 learners for success in their exams.
